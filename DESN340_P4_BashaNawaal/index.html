OUR STORY

Founded in 1971, Ptarmigan is a family-owned and operated woodworking company. Specializing in custom projects that are hand-crafted, timeless, and sustainable, Ptarmigan has expanded its services across Canada, becoming an industry leader in woodworking.

From the woods we source to the products we create, high-quality woods are standard materials
at Ptarmigan. Our woods are hand-picked from The Wood Shed local in Smithville, Ontario and have been
accredited by the Forestry Stewardship Council.
