OUR STORY

Founded in 1971, Ptarmigan is a family-owned and operated woodworking company. Specializing in custom projects that are hand-crafted, timeless, and sustainable, Ptarmigan has expanded its services across Alberta, becoming a local industry leader in woodworking.

From the woods we source to the products we create, high-quality woods are standard materials
at Ptarmigan. Our woods are hand-picked from The Wood Shed local in Smithville, Ontario and have been
accredited by the Forestry Stewardship Council.

WE MAKE CUSTOM WORKS

Our hand-crafted solutions to your ideas result in timeless customs to create your space or fill your space. We work together to design, create and produce work that we are proud of for folks we believe in. We are available for hire in a wide range of creative disciplines for a variety of jobs, projects and gigs.

SUSTAINABLE WOOD

As a local industry leader in woodworking, sustainability is one of the key drivers of our business. In collaboration with Coast to Coast Reforestation, Ptarmigan assists in supplying thousands of premium reforestation high-quality seedlings across Canada yearly.

CRAFTED TO BE TIMELESS

Built to withstand Alberta’s harshest weather conditions, Ptarmigan specializes in products that offer high-quality and timeless elegance. We are proud to provide our customers with products they can trust and will strive to ensure our clients are 100% satisfied.
